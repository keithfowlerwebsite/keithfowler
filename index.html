Hello and welcome to my pickleball coaching website. I am a PPO certified coach (rating level: pro coach) with a genuine passion for helping players who want to level up their skill in the game. Pickleball coaching combines two core aspects of who I am - sports and education - in a way that gives me great satisfaction, and helps me to be an effective coach. Since 2002, I have worked in the UK and US education systems as a teacher and administrator, with a masters in curriculum and instruction. Since infancy I’ve played various sports, including college-level soccer, and I am currently a competitive 4.0/4.5 level pickleball player. My full-time career as an educator helps me sequence and structure my coaching sessions and infuse them with best-practice instructional methods. My competitive sports experience means I instruct from the player’s perspective, understanding the desire to feel a sense of technical progress leading to more success on the court, and more overall enjoyment.
